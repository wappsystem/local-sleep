Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria


i. Inclusion Criteria

Community-dwelling

Age 35-65

PSG confirmed moderate to severe OSA based (AHI ≥15/hr)

Able to give informed consent

Fluent in English

CPAP or mandibular device naïve

Ability to perform neurobehavioural tests

Ability to perform driving simulator task

Current driver’s license

Performance impairment during a 30-minute driving simulation

Positive to questions 2 or 6 or 10 on ASTN MVA Questionnaire

Positive to question 8 of Epworth Sleepiness Scale (ESS)

Physician opinion: Positive for impairment

Willingness to use CPAP

ii. Exclusion Criteria

Clinically significant co-morbidity

Major neurological problems (e.g. stroke, epilepsy, head injury)

Severe mental health disorder(e.g. current major depression, schizophrenia, bipolar disorder)

Regular use of sleep-affecting medication; benzodiazepines, opioids, antidepressants

Shift worker

Have travelled overseas within the last 2 weeks

Professional driver

Eligibility

Eligible for study?
